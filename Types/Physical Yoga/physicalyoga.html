What is Physical Yoga?

According to the yoga help is the primal sacred duty of human being . The concept of the dehashuddhi , chittashudhi and atmashuddhi are very prominently deal with the in all system related to yoga . Hence a man is said to be healthy if he can enjoy the best physical efficiency greater immunity and greater biological and nervous control along with moral purity. On the other hand, disease is considered to be sin in yoga. And man is himself responsible for his health as well as disease. To maintain good health, healthy habits, exercise. Nutrition, diet, rest, hygiene, obsession from bad habits like smoking, drugs or excessive drinking and developing good social skills is necessary . Benefits Yoga is an ancient form of exercise that focuses on strength, flexibility and breathing to boost physical and mental wellbeing. The main components of yoga are postures a series of movements designed to increase strength and flexibility and breathing. The practice originated in India about 5,000 years ago and has been adapted in other countries in a variety of ways. Yoga is now commonplace in leisure centres, health clubs, schools, hospitals and surgeries.
